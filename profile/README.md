# Nexus

Welcome to Nexus, your ultimate companion for discovering and redeeming exclusive rewards in your favorite gacha games! Nexus is your dedicated Discord bot designed for gamers, delivering the latest redeem codes from accredited community sites and trusted community members in near-real-time.

## Features
- **Real-time Code Updates**: Get the latest redeem codes as soon as they are available.
- **Always Free**: Nexus is and will always be free to use. No paywalls, no locked content.
- **Community Driven**: Support Nexus through [GitHub Sponsorships](https://tbd.com). Your sponsorship helps us maintain and improve Nexus.
- **Continuous Improvement**: We strive to provide the best possible experience for all gamers.

## Get Started
To add Nexus to your Discord server and start redeeming codes, [invite the bot to your server](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1224387094831562902&permissions=142336&integration_type=0&scope=applications.commands+bot).
Then, visit our [website](https://nexus-codes.app) for more information and details about commands and features.
